# Privacy Policy for InstaFocus

**Last Updated: January 1, 2025**

## Overview

InstaFocus ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

InstaFocus operates entirely on your local device and does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your browsing history
- Store any data on external servers
- Share any information with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking tools

## Data Storage

The Extension stores only the following information locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Extension enabled/disabled state | Remember your preference |
| Feature toggles (ads, suggestions, debug) | Remember your settings |
| Blocked items count (session only) | Display statistics |

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted over the internet
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions

The Extension requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your settings locally |
| `activeTab` | Detect and hide ads on the current Instagram tab |
| `host_permissions` (instagram.com) | Operate only on Instagram pages |

## Third-Party Services

InstaFocus does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.
